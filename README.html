Thanks for downloading A Vaila Ball (version 1.0.2)

A Vaila Ball is a platform where you can play with your friends. What this game needs is your support. Levels may seem hard, but you need to push forward to put yourselves into leaderboards.

TUTORIAL

  • In order to complete a level: You need to collect all coins in the time period that you are given.
  • You can move by using: ←↑→↓ or WASD.
  • To go right: → or D
  • To go left: ← or A
  • To jump: ↑ or W or Space
  • To stop: ↓ or S
  • Double Jump: You can jump twice but you have to jump once in order to jump again. It means that if you start falling before you can jump, you can't jump twice.

IMPORTANT NOTES ABOUT THE INTERNET CONNECTION

  • What this game needs is a stable internet connection. Please check if you are connected to the internet before playing the game.
  • If you are not connected to the internet, you progress cannot be saved to the database.
  • If you happen to lost your internet connection after you complete a level, please wait until you are connected to the internet again. (So that your progress can be saved to the database)
